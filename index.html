About Me

A Passion for Perfect Fit

Namaste! I'm Ms. Sumalatha, a fashion tailor with over 15 years of experience weaving elegance into every stitch. Based in Hyderabad, I blend the richness of traditional Indian craftsmanship with the clean lines of contemporary fashion.

From the intricate embroidery of a bridal lehenga to the crisp tailoring of a modern blazer, every garment I create is made with intention, precision, and a deep respect for the person wearing it.

I believe clothing is a language — let me help you say exactly what you mean.
